About

Where We Came From

From classroom discussions to late night group meetings, our project evolved into a collaborative mission. Slumbr isn't just a product of our creativity and design class; it's a testament to our collective determination to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those affected by insomnia.


A Project Turned Personal

Late nights and brainstorming sessions fueled our curiosity, leading us to explore ways to aid those struggling with sleep. Each of us brought a unique perspective to the table, blending research, creativity, and a lot of trial and error. That's when the idea for Slumbr, our functional beverage, began to take shape.

A Life Changing Drink

In our pursuit to understand sleep-related struggles, we encountered individuals whose lives were deeply affected by the relentless grip of insomnia. Conversations with them illuminated the impact sleep had on their daily lives As we listened to their stories, a realization dawned upon us: our endeavor wasn't merely about creating a beverage; it was about improving lives. Witnessing firsthand the struggles these individuals faced each night fueled our determination. We found our vision in their narratives, recognizing the potential of Slumbr not just as a product but as a catalyst for positive change in the lives of those seeking sleep.



A Vision for the Future

Our aspirations for Slumbr extend far beyond a classroom project. We envision Slumbr as a beacon of hope for anyone wrestling with sleepless nights, aiming to reach households worldwide. We aim to expand our understanding and refining our product to better address various sleep-related challenges. Our vision encompasses not just a functional beverage but an entire ecosystem of support. Ultimately, we envision Slumbr as a trusted companion on the journey to a more rejuvenating and fulfilling night's sleep for all.
